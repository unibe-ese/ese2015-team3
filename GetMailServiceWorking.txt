Add to your beans in springData.xml:

<bean id="mailSender" class="org.springframework.mail.javamail.JavaMailSenderImpl">
        <property name="host" value="SMTP.OPENMAILBOX.ORG"/>
        <property name="port" value="587"/>
        <property name="username" value="tutoris.baernae@openmailbox.org"/>
        <property name="password" value="scel13"/>
        <property name="javaMailProperties">
            <props>
                <!-- Use SMTP transport protocol -->
                <prop key="mail.transport.protocol">smtp</prop>
                <!-- Use SMTP-AUTH to authenticate to SMTP server -->
                <prop key="mail.smtp.auth">true</prop>
                <!-- Use TLS to encrypt communication with SMTP server -->
                <prop key="mail.smtp.starttls.enable">true</prop>
            </props>
        </property>
    </bean>

<bean id="mailService" class="org.sample.controller.service.MailService">
    <property name="mailSender" ref="mailSender"/>
</bean>

Assure that your users have valid email addresses (or just ignore it if an error occurs in sending mails)
